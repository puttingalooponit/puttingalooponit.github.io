Nominal GDP's around the World & The Impact of Covid-19

Dealing with Covid-19 has proven to be a unique financial challenge globally, and so in this webpage, I attempt to paint a picture of the global economies pre-pandemic and also show the impact of Covid-19 on their growth.

The Size of the World's Economy

This visualisation represents the Nominal GDP by Volume of all the countries & Regions over the last decade.

Comparing the Financial Crises: Covid-19 vs The Great Recession

In this visualisation, we can see that among the most documented and developed economies of the world, the economic loss brought by Covid-19 is magnitudes greater then that of the The Great Recession. A frightening factor to consider is that with countries jumping in and out of lockdowns, the worst may be yet to come.
